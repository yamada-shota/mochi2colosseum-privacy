Mochi2 Colosseum offers optional in-app purchases (coins and cosmetic items). All transactions are handled by Apple, and the developer has no access to your payment information.

Mochi2 Colosseum does not use any advertising networks or third-party analytics SDKs. No data is shared with third parties beyond what is required to operate the leaderboard.

Mochi2 Colosseum does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
