Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Mochi2 Colosseum is an independently developed iOS game. Please read the following privacy policy before using the app.

Data Collection

To prevent cheating and ensure fair play, the following game data is stored on a secure cloud server (AWS / Amazon DynamoDB) linked to your Apple Game Center ID:

  • Game Center player ID (issued by Apple — the developer cannot see your Apple ID or real name)
  • Display name you set in the game
  • Score history and personal best
  • Coins, gems, stamina, owned skins, fields, and items

Settings and temporary UI state are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to a server.

Leaderboard

Your display name and score are stored on the server and shown in the public leaderboard. This data is never used for advertising or analytics.

In-App Purchase

Mochi2 Colosseum offers optional in-app purchases (coins and cosmetic items). All transactions are handled by Apple, and the developer has no access to your payment information.

Third Parties

Mochi2 Colosseum does not use any advertising networks or third-party analytics SDKs. No data is shared with third parties beyond what is required to operate the leaderboard.

Children's Privacy

Mochi2 Colosseum does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the App Store review page.
